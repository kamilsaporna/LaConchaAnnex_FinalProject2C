Origins of La Concha Restaurant

La Concha Restaurant traces its humble beginnings to a small cafeteria established by Lola Concha, the mother of our first owner. With a passion for cooking and a deep love for Filipino food, Lola Concha opened her doors to the community, serving delicious homemade pancit. It didn't take long for the locals to discover the exceptional taste of her signature dish, "Pancit Chami." Driven by the overwhelming response to the Pancit Chami, the small cafeteria flourished, becoming a beloved gathering place for both locals and visitors.

We invite you to join us at La Concha Annex Restaurant and experience the authentic taste of the Marinduqueno. Whether you're a long-time aficionado of Filipino cuisine or new to its flavors, our warm and welcoming ambiance, paired with our delicious dishes, promises an unforgettable dining experience.

Come, savor the essence of the Marinduque at La Concha Annex Restaurant.
